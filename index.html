Topic Background

The social issue that this project is centered around is reproductive rights. More specifically, we wanted to focus on abortion rights for women. The overturning of the Supreme Court case Roe v. Wade has affected many women across the United States, especially women of color or low-income women who live in states that have now banned abortion. Thus, we hope to use a motorized storyboard to depict the negative results of banning abortions.
